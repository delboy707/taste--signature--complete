## ðŸ“Š Step 2: Log Your First Product Experience (3 minutes)

Click **"Log Experience"** in the navigation menu.

### Fill out the 7-stage form:

**Stage 1: Product Details**
- Product name (e.g., "Dark Chocolate Bar 70%")
- Category (e.g., "Chocolate")
- Consumption occasion (e.g., "Afternoon snack")

**Stage 2: Appearance**
- Visual observations: color, texture, surface
- First impressions before tasting

**Stage 3: Aroma**
- What do you smell? (fruity, nutty, floral, etc.)
- Intensity and character

**Stage 4: Front Mouth (First Bite)**
- Initial taste impressions
- Texture on first contact

**Stage 5: Mid/Rear Mouth (During Chewing)**
- Flavor development
- Textural changes
- Taste evolution

**Stage 6: Aftertaste**
- What lingers after swallowing?
- Duration and quality

**Stage 7: Emotional Response**
- How does this product make you feel?
- What mood or memory does it evoke?

Click **"Save Experience"** when done!
